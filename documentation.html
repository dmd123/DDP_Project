PJM LMP Data

This application is a tool for viewing realtime electricity pricing for the PJM Interconnection. The PJM Interconnectionn is a regional transmission organization that includes all or parts of Delaware, Illinois, Indiana, Kentucky, Maryland, Michigan, New Jersey, North Carolina, Ohio, Pennsylvania, Tennessee, Virginia, West Virginia, and the District of Columbia. It's operations include managing electricity transmission and operating a wholesale electricity market for this region. Electricity prices for 5 minutes intervals are available on its website.
